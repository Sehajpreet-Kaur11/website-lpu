About Us

Shaping Future Leaders
Since 2005

Lovely Professional University (LPU) is India's largest private university, situated on a sprawling 600-acre campus in Phagwara, Punjab. Since its inception, LPU has consistently pushed the boundaries of academic excellence and innovation.

Our institution blends rigorous academics with real-world industry experience, ensuring every graduate is not just employable — but extraordinary. We house state-of-the-art labs, innovation hubs, sports arenas, and research centers that rival global standards.

With a diverse community from 50+ countries, LPU is truly a global campus that prepares students to thrive in an interconnected world.

FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

What boards/programs does LPU follow?

+

LPU offers UGC-recognized degrees across Engineering, Management, Design, Sciences, Law, and more. We are NAAC A++ accredited and ranked by NIRF. We also offer integrated NPTEL certifications, industry partnerships, and international exchange programs from Year 1.

What is the admission process?

+

Submit the online application form, followed by a LPUNEST entrance exam or valid score (JEE/CLAT/NATA etc.), and a personal interaction. Admissions are merit-based with consideration for holistic development. Scholarships are available for meritorious students.

What extracurricular activities are available?

+

LPU offers 200+ clubs including robotics, debate, music, art, coding, chess, yoga, swimming, cricket, theatre arts, and entrepreneurship cells. The campus hosts 600+ events annually with dedicated coaches and infrastructure.

What are the hostel and campus facilities?

+

LPU's 600-acre campus includes fully furnished hostels for boys and girls with 24/7 security, Wi-Fi, gyms, and canteens. The campus has hospitals, banks, shopping areas, cinema, and sports complexes — a complete township for students.

How is the placement record?

+

LPU maintains a 93%+ placement rate with over 600+ companies visiting campus annually including Google, Amazon, Microsoft, Infosys, TCS, Wipro, and ISRO. The highest package offered is ₹3 Crore per annum. Our dedicated placement cell works year-round for student success.

Are there scholarships available?

+

Yes, LPU offers extensive scholarships including merit-based (up to 100% tuition waiver), sports, differently-abled, and defence quota scholarships. Students from economically weaker sections can also apply for government scholarships applicable at LPU.
